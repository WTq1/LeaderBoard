Problem 9 - Average Acceleration

To find the average acceleration of the car, we can use the following formula:

Acceleration = (Final Velocity - Initial Velocity) / Time

Given that the car accelerates from rest (initial velocity = 0 m/s) to a velocity of 20 m/s in 10 seconds, we can substitute these values into the formula:

Acceleration = (20 - 0) / 10

Acceleration = 20 / 10

Acceleration = 2 m/s²

Answer: The car's average acceleration is 2 m/s².
