Problem 1 - Friends' Ages

Let's solve the problem step by step.

Let the age of the first friend be x years.

The second friend is twice as old as the first friend, so the age of the second friend is 2x years.

The third friend is three times as old as the first friend, so the age of the third friend is 3x years.

The sum of their ages is 60, so we have the equation:

x + 2x + 3x = 60

Solving the equation:

6x = 60

x = 10

Therefore, the ages of the three friends are:

First friend: 10 years

Second friend: 20 years

Third friend: 30 years

Answer: The ages of the three friends are 10, 20, and 30 years respectively.
